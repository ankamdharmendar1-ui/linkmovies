Say Hello!

At MoviesFun, our visitors are at the heart of everything we do. We genuinely believe that building a great platform is impossible without listening to the people who use it every day. Whether you have a burning movie request, spotted a broken video link that needs fixing, or just want to share some kind words about your experience on our site - our inbox is always, always open.

We are a small, dedicated team working hard behind the scenes to curate the best Hindi dubbed movies for you. Because our team is small, we ask for just a little bit of patience when you reach out. We read absolutely every single email and message we receive. It might take us a little time to reply, but we promise that your voice will be heard.

For general inquiries, feedback, and movie requests, we typically aim to respond within 24 to 48 hours during regular working days. If you are reaching out regarding an urgent copyright matter, please follow the specific instructions on our DMCA page to ensure your request is routed to the correct department and handled as swiftly as possible.

What Can You Write About?

Sometimes staring at a blank email can be daunting. If you are wondering whether your message is appropriate or if it's something we want to hear about, the answer is almost certainly yes! Here are some of the most common reasons our visitors reach out to us:

1. Requesting a Specific Movie to be Added
This is our favorite type of email! If you know of a great movie that has been officially uploaded to YouTube in Hindi but isn't on MoviesFun yet, send us the title (and the link, if you have it). We love discovering new gems alongside our community.

2. Reporting a Broken Video Link
Because we link to third-party content on YouTube, videos can sometimes be made private, restricted, or removed by the uploader. While we run regular checks, we cannot catch every broken link instantly. If you click on a movie and see an error, shoot us a quick email. We will update or replace the link as soon as possible.

3. Sharing Feedback on the Website Experience
Is a button not working? Does the site look weird on your specific phone model? Or maybe you have a brilliant idea for a new feature that would make browsing easier? We want to hear it. Constructive criticism is the only way we improve.

4. Business Partnerships or Collaborations
Are you a content creator, a representative of a movie production house, or looking to collaborate in a way that benefits Hindi movie fans? We are always open to exploring genuine, mutually beneficial partnerships. Reach out to our general inbox with a clear subject line.

5. Just Saying You Love the Site!
Building and maintaining a website takes a lot of late nights and hard work. Hearing that our efforts are helping you discover great entertainment and relax after a long day truly makes it all worthwhile. Never hesitate to send a little positivity our way!

A Quick Note Before You Hit Send

To help us serve you better, please keep your emails clear and concise. If you are reporting a technical issue or a broken movie link, please include the exact URL of the page you were on when the issue occurred. If you are requesting a movie, please provide the full correct title, and if possible, the release year and lead actors, so we can be sure we are looking for the right film.

Please remember that MoviesFun is a free discovery platform. We do not charge subscriptions, and we do not host files. We simply link to publicly available content on YouTube. We kindly ask that you keep all communications respectful and polite. Abusive, spammy, or offensive emails will be filtered out and will not receive a response.

Thank you for being a part of the MoviesFun community. We look forward to hearing from you!
