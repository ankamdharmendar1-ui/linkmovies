DMCA Policy

We take copyright seriously — here's how we handle it

We Don't Host Any Videos

Let's be clear about one thing right away — MoviesFun does not upload, store, or host any video files on its own servers. Everything you watch on our site plays directly from YouTube, which means the actual video lives on YouTube's servers, not ours.

Think of us like a library catalogue — we list and link to publicly available content, but the books (videos) live somewhere else entirely. This is an important distinction when it comes to copyright matters.

How to File a DMCA Takedown

If you're a copyright owner and believe your content has been linked here without permission, here's exactly what you should do:

1
File with YouTube first. Since the video lives on YouTube, submit a DMCA notice directly to YouTube. Once they remove it, the link on our site automatically breaks.
2
Email us to remove the listing. If you'd also like us to remove the movie card from our directory, email dmca@moviesfun.example with your name, the URL on our site, and proof of copyright ownership.
3
We'll act quickly. Once we verify your claim, we'll remove the listing within 48 hours. We have zero tolerance for copyright violations and want to work with rightsholders, not against them.
