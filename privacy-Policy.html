Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Our Privacy Philosophy

At MoviesFun, we believe that your privacy is absolutely fundamental. We know that nobody likes feeling tracked, monitored, or having their personal data sold off to the highest bidder. When you visit our platform to look for a great Hindi dubbed movie, you should be able to do so with peace of mind. We have designed this Privacy Policy to be as clear, transparent, and easy to understand as possible, without confusing legal jargon.

This document explains exactly what information we collect when you visit MoviesFun, why we collect it, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By continuing to use and navigate our website, you are agreeing to the practices described in this policy. If you ever have any questions about how your data is handled, you are always welcome to contact us.

Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. This means we only collect the absolute minimum amount of information necessary to keep our website running smoothly, fix technical issues, and understand generally what kinds of movies our audience enjoys. We do not require you to create an account, register with an email address, or provide any identifying personal details just to browse our site.

The information we do collect falls primarily into two categories:

  • Non-Personal Identifying Information: Like most standard websites, we use basic analytics tools to automatically collect certain technical information when you visit. This includes your browser type (e.g., Chrome, Safari), your device type (Mobile vs. Desktop), your general geographic location (like your country or city, but never your specific address), and the referring website that brought you to us. This data is entirely anonymous and cannot be tied back to you as an individual.
  • Information You Voluntarily Provide: If you choose to contact us via email - whether to request a movie, report a broken link, or submit a DMCA notice - we will naturally receive your email address and any other information you choose to include in your message. We use this information solely for the purpose of replying to your specific inquiry.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Yes, MoviesFun uses cookies. But before you get worried, let's explain what they are and how we use them. A cookie is simply a tiny text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit. It allows the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as your preferred language or display settings) over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

We use cookies primarily for analytical purposes. For example, we use services like Google Analytics to understand which movies are the most popular, which pages take too long to load, and how users navigate through our site. This helps us dramatically improve the layout and content of MoviesFun.

Third-Party Cookies: Because we embed videos from YouTube, YouTube (a Google company) may also place cookies on your device to track your viewing preferences, remember your playback settings, or gather data for targeted advertising on their platform. We do not have control over these third-party cookies. We highly recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data collected through embedded videos.

If you prefer not to accept cookies, you can easily change your web browser settings to refuse them or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. Just keep in mind that disabling cookies might cause certain minor features of our website to function improperly.

How We Share Your Information

This section is very simple: We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties for marketing purposes. Never.

We may share anonymous, aggregated data (like the total number of visitors we received last month) with potential partners, but this data contains absolutely nothing that could identify you personally.

The only rare exception to this rule is if we are required to disclose information to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety. For instance, if we receive a legally binding court order, we are obligated to comply.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MoviesFun is designed for a general audience. However, we are fully committed to protecting the privacy of young children on the internet. We do not knowingly collect, maintain, or use personal information from children under the age of 13, and no part of our website is structured specifically to attract anyone under 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information (such as sending us an email), we will immediately and permanently delete that information from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take immediate action.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

The internet moves fast, and laws surrounding digital privacy are constantly evolving. As such, we may need to update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, new features on our website, or new legal requirements. When we do make changes, we will post the updated policy right here on this page and update the modification date at the bottom.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically so you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we may disclose it.
