Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026 — We keep this simple and honest

The Short Version

We know nobody enjoys reading long legal documents. So here's the honest, plain-English version — MoviesFun does not collect your personal information, does not sell your data, and does not require you to create an account. You can browse and enjoy movies completely anonymously.

That's genuinely our approach. We built this site to be a tool for movie lovers, not a data harvesting machine.

What We Don't Collect

We want to be completely transparent. Here's what we do NOT collect from you:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location or IP address (for tracking purposes)
  • Your payment or banking information
  • Your watch history or personal preferences stored on our servers

Cookies & YouTube

We may use basic browser cookies to remember small preferences like your selected genre filter. These are stored only on your own device and never sent to us. You can clear them anytime through your browser settings.

However, since we embed YouTube videos, please note that YouTube has its own tracking and cookie policies. When you play a video, you're interacting with YouTube's player, which operates under Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact About Privacy

If you have any questions about your privacy on MoviesFun, feel free to reach out to us at privacy@moviesfun.example. We'll respond within 48 hours.
